Nantucket Cranberry Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cranberries
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1 cup pecans or walnuts
  • Flour
  • 2 eggs
  • More sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. coat pie pan with butter
  2. Coarsely chop cranberries and cover the bottom of the pie pan with them
  3. Cover the cranberries with sugar
  4. Chop the nuts and cover te cranberries
  5. Make the cake dough
  6. Pour the dough into the pie pan
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes
