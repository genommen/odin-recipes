Mango Chia Pudding

Ingredients

  • Chia Seeds
  • 1 cup fresh chopped mango
  • 3/4 cup almond milk
  • 3/4 cup coconut cream
  • 1 TBsp Agave nectar

Steps

  1. Mix the chia seeds, almond milk, agave nectar, and coconut cream
  2. Chill overnight
  3. Puree the mango
  4. Split chilled mixture into 2 cups
  5. Mix half of the mango puree into each cup
